Creating Super Mentor page

Duplicate Mentors page and rename it SuperMentors.

Do the following changes
1.    SuperMentor Slicer

           


1a. In the fields, open ‘ADF_Mentorship_Activity’ Table, drag the SuperMentor and remove MM_Mentor.
1b. In the Formatting Tab-->General-->Title: SuperMentor
 
2.    Creating textbox for SuperMentor selected

         


2a. Click on Mentor textbox and drag the MMSuperMentor measure to the fields value box.

3.     Go to KPI Cards for the 2nd Main KPI (No of SuperM)
3a. Drag SuperMentor column to the fields value box from ADF_Mentorship_Activity, right click and select distinct Count.
3b. In the Formatting Tab-->General-->Properties-->Title: No of SuperM.

4a. Click on Mentor|On Pace Scatter chart and change the following on values drag and add SuperMentor at the top of MM-Mentor, MM_Student, remove CurrSection_Phase1 
4b. Drag the following measures into the Tooltips field: (LastRunDate, MM_Mentor, MentorScoreAvg%, Monthly Placement)
4c. In the Formatting Tab-->General--> Title: Super Mentor | On Pace

      Please see the below gif for guidance.

5a. In the Formatting Tab--> Button: Style --> Text:  Color = #808080

Effects-->Background: On, Color = Black, Shadow= On
